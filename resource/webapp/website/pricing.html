Frequently asked questions

Is there really no long-term contract? +

Correct — all DealerOS plans are month-to-month. Cancel any time from your dashboard with no fees or penalties. We earn your business every month on merit.

How long does it take to go live? +

Most dealerships go fully live within 24 hours of signing up. Our onboarding team handles inventory import, design setup, and DNS configuration — no developer needed.

Can I migrate from my current provider? +

Yes. We have a dedicated migration team handling transfers from CDK, DealerSocket, Dealer.com, and most other platforms. Your inventory, leads, and content migrate seamlessly.

What DMS systems do you integrate with? +

DealerOS integrates natively with CDK Global, Reynolds & Reynolds, DealerSocket, Dealertrack, and 15+ other DMS platforms. Custom integrations available via REST API for Enterprise customers.

How does the 60-day guarantee work? +

If you don't generate more leads in your first 60 days, contact support. We'll issue a full refund of your first two months — no questions, no hassle, no conditions.

Are there any per-lead or per-click fees? +

Never. DealerOS is 100% flat-rate. No per-lead, per-click, or per-vehicle fees. The price you see is the price you pay — full stop.
